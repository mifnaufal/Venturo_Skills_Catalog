# Growth — Ocean Depths

A professional maritime theme with deep navy and teal for trust-building.

## Color Palette

- **Primary Accent**: `#006D79` (Venturo teal)
- **Deep Navy**: `#1a2332` — Headings, strong elements
- **Teal**: `#2d8b8b` — Feature checkmarks, accents
- **Seafoam**: `#a8dadc` — Light backgrounds, subtle sections
- **Cream**: `#f1faee` — Main background

## Typography

- **Headers**: Use 'Outfit' bold
- **Body Text**: Use 'InstrumentSans' or 'Inter'

## Visual Treatment

- Cream to seafoam gradient background
- Deep navy headings for corporate professionalism
- Teal checkmark circles, slightly larger than default
- Subtle wave-like curved divider between features and meta-grid
- Clean, corporate feel with ocean serenity
