# Enterprise — Midnight Galaxy

Dramatic cosmic dark mode with deep purples and neon teal — perfect for enterprise-level solutions.

## Color Palette

- **Primary Accent**: `#006D79` (Venturo teal)
- **Deep Purple**: `#2b1e3e` — Main background
- **Cosmic Blue**: `#4a4e8f` — Secondary background, gradients
- **Lavender**: `#a490c2` — Subtle accents, borders
- **Silver**: `#e6e6fa` — Headings, primary text
- **Teal glow**: `#006D79` with box-shadow glow for brand elements

## Typography

- **Headers**: 'BricolageGrotesque' bold from canvas-fonts
- **Body Text**: 'InstrumentSans' or 'Inter'

## Visual Treatment

- Deep purple-to-cosmic blue gradient background (#2b1e3e → #4a4e8f)
- Radial-gradient nebula effects in background (purple + teal)
- Neon teal glow on Venturo brand name
- Checkmarks have lavender outline circles with cosmic blue fill
- Tech-mono font for timeline and team spec labels
- Very subtle star/dot pattern as background texture
- Silver headings with slight text shadow for depth
